Abar ƙaunata, dafatan kintashi lafiya? Duk safiya Ina tashi da Muradi uku ne a zuciyata: na farko shine ganin murmushinki mai ƙayatarwa, ganin ƙyakkyawar fuskarki mai sani farin ciki ko da yaushe, na karshe kuma saki farin ciki a koda yaushe.
A kullin natashi daga bacci, sai inji nishadi sosai ba dan komai ba, sai dan samun tauraruwa irinki a rayuwa na. 
Kintashi daga bacci kuwa? Rana ta fito, tsuntsaye nata rera waka mai matukar ƙayatarwa dan su sanar da masoyiyata gari ya waye. Ki kulamun da kanki.
A rayuwa na bansan tashi da safe, amma samun ki arayuwa na yasa tashi da safe domin jin muryanki yazama muradina. Jin kina lafiya nasani nishadi da jindadi.
Turamaki sakonni na soyayya a kullin da safe ya zama abu mafi karfi a zuciyata kuma makutar jiki da rai, bazan fasa turamaki sakonni ba. Soboda farin cikinki shine tawa.
Sarauniyar kyawu! Natashi yau da safe na rasa me ke damuna, sai daga baya zuciyata ke ce mun; baka gaishe da abar kaunarka ba. Dafatan kintashi lpy sarauniyata!!
Kullin inna tashi daga bacci, in wayata ta buga, sai inyi tunanin sakonki yazo. Har ƙosa wa nake gari ya waye inganki zuciyata tasamu natsuwa da nishadi.
Kamar yadda nake maki tunaninki duk lafiya, haka nakeso kizama wacce zanfara gani koda yaushe innatashi daga bacci, zaki iya yin mun hakan?
Ki tashi, safiya tayi. A kullin ina kara jindadi da alfahari soboda samunki a matsayin me sani nishadi da farin ciki kuma matukar akwai rai, hakan bazata sulwanta ba.
Kowani safiya agare ni, dama ce na kara karfafa Kula da soyayyata agareki. Nidai fata na shine kamar yadda ubangiji yahadamu, ya barmu tare mu rayu cikin nishadi da annashuwa, Ina kewarki.
Ina kwananki! Kullin cikin idanunki da kuma mafarkin danayi game fake, na fahimci kewa taimakonki da kuma soyayyarki gareni abu ne Wanda kika dauka da mahimmanci, soboda haka, bazan taba bari kiyi kasa agwiwa ba.
Barka da safiya ga sarauniyar dake rike da makullin zuciyata! Dafatan kintashi lafiya? Duk safiya, ina tashi ne da muradin ganin wayata rana kinzama tawa nikadai, yadda komai naki zai dawo hannuna. Hakan abun alfaharine awurina.
Babban bakin cikina shine in tashi da safe ban turamaki sakonni ba, haka kuma inna turamaki, hakan samamun nishadi yake fiye da yadda nake tinani.
Dafatan kin kwana cikin koshin lafiya? Sarauniyata ki tashi, kinsan safiya bata cika batare dake ba a zuciyata.
Kinsan meyasa duk safiya nake jindadin turamaki sakonni? Dan kisan da tunaninki nake kwana a zuciyata kuma dashi nake tashi, ko hakan zai sa ki kisan yadda nake ƙaunarki a zuciyata.
Hasken da ke haskakawa daga gare ki ya fi hasken rana mahimmanci a gare ni. Tashi ki haskaka, kyakkyawar sarauniyata. 
Ke ce kawai yarinya a duniya a gare ni, kuma duk ranar da duniya ta juya ta fuskanci rana, ina farin ciki da na tashi tare da ke. Barka da safiya, kyakkyawan fure na!
Yayin da nake sauraren bututun ruwan sama a kan kwano, murmushin ki shine hasken safiya na. Ina son ki,  masoyiyata. 
Hasken idanunki yayin da kike kallona kamar kallon fitowar rana ne. Ba zan iya neman nesa ba na daƙiƙa guda. Rana ta mai da hanyoyin mu zinariya a yau. 
Wani kyakkyawan safiya da zan kwana tare da ke, uwargidana kyakkyawa. 
Allah ya kara mana wata rana mai albarka fiye da yadda zamu iya lissafawa. Duk da haka, bari mu gwada shi ta wata hanya. Barka da safiya My Love! 
Tashi da rike ki a hannuna kowace safiya tamkar mafarki ne wanda ba zan taba so ya kare ba. Barka da safiya, amaryata.
Ina sa ran abubuwa uku daga gare ki kowace safiya, gimbiyata: dariya, so, da latti. Kina yin mafi kyawun lattes.
Kowane dare na kan kwanta barci ina fatan mafarkin ki masu dadi, amma lokacin da na tashi, na yi farin ciki cewa ƙaunarmu gaskiya ce.
Gaskiya naji dadin iya kiranki masoyiyata. Barka da safiya, kyakkyawa. Ina fatan kina da babbar rana. 
Ban taba tunanin soyayyar gaskiya ta wanzu ba sai ranar da na kamu da sonki. Barka da safiya, ji daɗin ranarki masoyiyana.
Duk safiya da na tashi kusa da ke, ita ce wacce zan ƙaunace ta har abada. Ba zan tashe ki ba; ji dadin ranar ki. Tashi ki haskaka, Abun ƙaunata.
Rana ta fito, kuma tsuntsaye suna rera waƙa don su sanar da ke girman safiya. Duk daren da muka rabu ina mafarkin ki. 
Ke mace ce mai ban mamaki da gaske, kuma ba zan iya jira in kira ki amarya ta ba. 
Suna cewa Allah ya halicci mata cikin surar mutum, amma kyawunki ya wuce yarda ake tunani. Ji daɗin ranar ki, rabin raina.
